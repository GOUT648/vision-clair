    What It Does

    EVERY FEATURE
    YOU NEED

    From real-time gesture recognition to an embedded AI voice assistant — CLAIR is a complete sign language ecosystem, not just a detector.

    01
    Real-Time Recognition

    Detects ASL and BSL fingerspelling at 30 FPS via MediaPipe landmark tracking. Works on any webcam, no special hardware needed.

    02
    Adaptive Learning

    Trains silently on your exact signing style every session. The model personalises to your hand shape and improves over time.

    03
    AI Voice Engine

    Speaks your signed sentences through ElevenLabs expressive TTS or local pyttsx3. Polished by Gemini before output.

    04
    Air Drawing

    Pinch to draw directly on the camera feed in real time. Multi-color strokes, smooth curves, fully gesture-controlled.

    05
    Teach Mode

    Press T to open guided teaching. Show any gesture, label it, and it learns immediately. Build custom sign packs for any language.

    06
    Plugin System

    Drop a .json gesture pack into the plugins folder. Auto hot-reloaded every 5 seconds — zero restarts, zero code changes.

    Under The Hood

    HOW IT
    WORKS

    01
    Camera Input

    MediaPipe detects 21 hand landmarks per hand at 30 FPS with sub-5ms latency on CPU.

    02
    Feature Extraction

    167 normalised geometric features are computed — hand-size independent, rotation robust.

    03
    Dual Inference

    Rule-based scorer boots instantly. Adaptive SGD model takes over as it learns your style.

    04
    Word Builder + Voice

    Committed letters form words, polished by Gemini, spoken aloud through ElevenLabs or local TTS.
